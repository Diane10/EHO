Welcome to Equitable Health Organization

EHO is a non governmental organization which is looking forward to advocating for the rights, invest in the education and healthcare of disabled children to ensure proper treatment. We are looking forward to building confidence in people with disabilities and giving them room for showing and practicing what they can once they are given space. EHO also organizes multiple summits and training about human rights and social inclusion to change the stereotype status quo of what other people see when they hear and see the disabled person.

Our organization depends on the generosity of donors and volunteers to achieve our goals. There are different ways of supporting our cause and we welcome any help we can get . Donate to Campaignsor Volunteer Online Contact us.
